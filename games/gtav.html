About Game

Step into the sprawling, sun-soaked city of Los Santos and the surrounding Blaine County in one of the most immersive open-world games ever made. Play as three unique characters—Michael, Franklin, and Trevor—each with their own stories, skills, and missions.

Explore a world packed with heists, street races, businesses to run, and countless side activities. Drive exotic cars, fly jets, dive into the ocean, or just cause chaos—the choice is yours.
